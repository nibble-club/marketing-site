Did you know?

U.S. restaurants generate an estimated 22 to 33 billion pounds of food waste each year, largely driven by over-preparation of food, extensive menu choices, and fluctuation in sales.

-Natural Resources Defense Council

The restaurant industry spends an estimated $162 billion every year in costs related to wasted food.

-Environmental Protection Agency

A 2017 study found that the median restaurant sees more than an 8-fold financial return on investment in food waste reduction efforts.

-The Business Case for Reducing Food Loss and Waste Report

Introducing Nibble

At Nibble, we believe that doing good can be a win-win for everyone.

We connect high quality restaurants that have extra food with paying customers looking to save money on takeout. Customers subscribe to our service and can cash in on the meals available near them at any time. Restaurants get paid for adding their extra foods to the platform instead of tossing money down the drain.
